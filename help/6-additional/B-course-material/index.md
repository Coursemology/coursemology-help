To manage your course materials, you can organize them into subfolders.
  1. GOTO: **Materials > New Subfolder**
  2. Input the details accordingly and **Submit**.

![6B-1](/images/6B-1.gif)

To upload files,
  1. Navigate into the specific subfolder
  2. **Upload** the file.
